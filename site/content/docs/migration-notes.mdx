The planning family now lives in the root `review-game-core` package.

Before that migration, the strongest precursor lived in the vendored stats copy. The migration kept the useful semantics and normalized the vocabulary.

## What moved into root core

- `GoalPlan`
- `GoalPhaseDefinition`
- `GoalPhaseSnapshot`
- `GoalPhaseState`
- `GoalPlanEvaluation`
- canonical deadline behavior and recommendation roles

## What stayed outside the core

- learner identity
- grade thresholds
- exam-pack naming and copy
- section-specific course policy
- launcher routing and CTA text

## Consumer shape after migration

- `stats-exam-prep-game` builds phase snapshots from concept progress
- `math-1111-review-game` builds phase snapshots from section progress
- both apps keep their own UI and recommendation framing

Use the [planning docs](/docs/planning-goals) and the [planning feature page](/showcase/features/planning-goals) together when migrating a new consumer.
